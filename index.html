Dog Breed Classifier

by Eduardo Morales Vizcarra


This tool helps you to identify and classify your dog breed if your dog is one of the following: Xoloitzquintle, Samoyed or Pomeranian. You need to upload an image of your dog and I will tell you which breed your dog is. If you upload an image of a dog from another breed, then, I will tell you which breed is much closer to.




In the next section, it describes the features for each dog breed with some samples of the image classifier. Also, we can observe a histogram representing the values of reds, greens, blues, whites, blacks and browns made with d3.


Xoloitzquintle

EXPLANATION:This histogram represents the values of reds, greens, blues, whites, blacks, and browns from one image of the whole sample of Xoloitzquintles. This graph uses the vectorized data of the color values of the image. With this information we can continue exploring the training and prediction for our dog breed image classifier.
