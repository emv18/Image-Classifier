Dog Breed Classifier

by Eduardo Morales Vizcarra


This tool helps you to identify and classify your dog breed if your dog is one of the following: Xoloitsquintle, Samoyed or Pomerania. You need to upload an image of your dog and I will tell you which breed your dog is. If you upload an image of a dog from another breed, then, I will tell you which breed is much closer to.




In the next section, it describes the features for each dog breed with some samples of the image classifier.
